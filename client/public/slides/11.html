Limitations & Future Work

Roadmap to Scalable Optimization

Current Limitations

Sensor Accuracy

Voltage/current sensors have ±2% variance which may cause marginal false triggers during rapid irradiance changes.

Maintenance Requirements

Solenoid valves and spray nozzles require annual inspection to prevent clogging and ensure durability.

Connectivity Reliability

Standard WiFi may be unstable in remote areas. Cellular backup improves reliability but increases cost.

+75 AED/section
Cemented Dust Handling

System handles standard dust density up to 3 g/m². Extreme caking requires manual intervention.
