Key Challenges

Problem Statement

Major factors undermining PV system performance in arid environments
01

Irregular Dust Events

102-154 days/year

High variability in dust storm patterns across UAE regions makes fixed-schedule maintenance ineffective. Eastern areas experience up to 154 dust days annually.

02

Excessive Water Usage

9-15 tons/MW

Traditional cleaning methods consume massive amounts of water per cycle. For a 2 MW installation, this creates significant sustainability concerns in water-scarce regions.

03

High Operational Costs

35.9% of total cost

Manual labor, equipment depreciation, and scheduled robot operations represent over one-third of total system costs over the operational lifetime, reducing ROI.

04

Suboptimal Energy Yield

67% capture efficiency

Delayed responses to dust events and unnecessary cleaning during stable periods result in significant lost revenue and extended payback periods.

Source: HelioMetrics Research Paper, p.2
