Key Challenges

Problem Statement

Major factors undermining PV system performance in arid environments
01

Irregular Dust Events

102–154 days/year

High variability in dust storm patterns across UAE regions makes fixed-schedule maintenance ineffective.

02

Excessive Water Usage

9–15 tons/MW

Traditional cleaning methods consume massive amounts of water, raising sustainability concerns.

03

High Operational Costs

35.9% of total cost

Labor, equipment wear, and robot operation significantly reduce ROI over system lifetime.

04

Suboptimal Energy Yield

67% capture efficiency

Delayed cleaning responses and unnecessary cycles cause avoidable energy losses.
